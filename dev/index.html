A Q&A with Our Go-To Beauty Guru

Cyndle
Komarovski

What trends are you most excited about for fall?

Red lipstick is feeling fresh again. To update this classic look, wear it without mascara. Instead, fill in your brows to frame your eyes or use a gel liner at the base of your lashes for a subtle contrast.

What’s your best tip for transitioning your makeup from summer to fall?

Good makeup starts with good skin care. Adjust your routine by adding in more moisture (think creams and oils) to combat the drying effects of indoor heating. You can also switch out your summer bronzer for a cream blush in a rosy tone for fall.

What’s the one thing you won’t leave the house without doing?

My eyebrows! You don't need to wear much makeup if your eyebrows are perfectly groomed.
